[v0.3]
Introduce new "Ideology" mechanic:
- Every 3 cycles (first time at the start of cycle 3, so it fits nicely into the tutorial), each party has gets 3 semi-random "Ideologies" to choose from. Ideologies are a set of passive bonuses and penalties that define the general direction of the party, and also give a bit of flavour. The player can see the ideologies of the other parties. The offered ideologies are somewhat random but also weighted based on the policies the party has been using up to this point.
- Ideas for first set of ideologies:
  - Ecologist: +1 impact, +8 cap for "Renewables" economic policiy. Cannot use "Fossil Fuels" economic policy (-8 cap).
  - Opportunist: +1 policy point per turn.
  - <RELIGION> Follower: +1 impact, +8 cap of <RELIGION> policy. Cannot use policies related to other religions.
  - <INDUSTRY> Lobbyist: +1 impact, +8 cap for <INDUSTRY> economic policy. -20 popularity in districts without that industry.
  - Minority Supporter: +X impact for all language and religion policies, where X = 5-[number of districts with that language or religion].
- Make sure the bot AI can also use the ideologies in a smart way. Doesn't have to be super complex. Since they already use weights for policies, it can just be an extra weight added to the relevant policies when they are evaluating them. And add a basic "score system" for ideologies, so they can choose the best one among the 3 offered based on their current policy usage.
- Add a new tab for this.
